Paris - Explore the City of Light

Eiffel Tower

The iconic symbol of Paris, standing 330 meters tall, was completed in 1889. Visit at sunset for breathtaking views of the city.

Visit the Official Eiffel Tower Website

Louvre Museum

Home to thousands of works of art including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo. The world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris.

Visit the Louvre Museum Website

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A medieval Catholic cathedral on the Île de la Cité. Known for its French Gothic architecture and its stunning stained glass windows.

Visit Notre-Dame Cathedral Website

Montmartre

A large hill in Paris, crowned by the Sacré-Coeur Basilica. Known for its artistic history and stunning views of the city.

Explore Montmartre

Champs-Élysées

One of the world's most famous avenues, stretching from Place de la Concorde to the Arc de Triomphe. Known for luxury shops, cafés, and theaters.

Learn More About Champs-Élysées

Seine River

The Seine flows through the heart of Paris. Take a river cruise to see many of the city's famous landmarks from a different perspective.

Discover the Seine River
